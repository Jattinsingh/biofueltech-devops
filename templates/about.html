About Us

BioFuelTech Energy is a renewable energy startup focused on converting organic waste into biogas. Our mission is to create sustainable energy solutions and reduce environmental pollution in India. BioFuelTech Energy introduces a scalable and modular approach to biogas production, which differentiates it from traditional fixed biogas systems.  The innovation lies in integrating waste collection logistics, modular plant design, and optional IoT-based monitoring systems to optimize gas production efficiency.  The startup focuses on reducing setup costs and improving accessibility by deploying small-scale units that can be replicated across multiple locations.  Additionally, the business model incorporates a structured distribution mechanism for biogas and organic fertilizer, enabling sustainable revenue generation and environmental impact. Our startup is different from normal biogas plants because:  We use modular (small & flexible) systems  Easy to install and low cost  Can be used in villages and cities  Future plan: use smart monitoring (IoT)
